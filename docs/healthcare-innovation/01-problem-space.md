# Problem Space

## Purpose

This document captures healthcare pain points, workflow failures, safety risks, and operational challenges that may be addressed through digital health, health informatics, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and workflow redesign.

---

# Patient Safety

## Retained Surgical Items

Description:
Objects such as surgical swabs, instruments, needles, and gloves may occasionally be left inside patients after procedures.

Impact:

* Patient harm
* Additional surgeries
* Legal liability
* Increased healthcare costs

Potential Solution Areas:

* Asset tracking
* Surgical count automation
* RFID systems
* Computer vision
* Workflow verification

---

## Wrong Patient / Wrong Procedure Events

Description:
Procedures performed on the wrong patient or incorrect body site.

Impact:

* Severe patient harm
* Reputational damage
* Litigation
* Regulatory investigations

Potential Solution Areas:

* Identity verification
* Barcode scanning
* Clinical workflow controls
* AI-assisted validation

---

## Medication Errors

Description:
Patients receive incorrect medication, incorrect dosage, incorrect route, or incorrect timing.

Impact:

* Adverse drug events
* Hospitalization
* Mortality
* Increased costs

Potential Solution Areas:

* Clinical decision support
* Smart medication verification
* Predictive alerts

---

# Information Management

## Duplicate Patient Records

Description:
The same patient may have multiple records across systems.

Impact:

* Missed information
* Delayed treatment
* Incorrect clinical decisions

Potential Solution Areas:

* Master patient index
* Identity management
* Record matching algorithms

---

## Missing Clinical Information

Description:
Critical information is unavailable, incomplete, delayed, or buried within records.

Impact:

* Delayed diagnosis
* Treatment errors
* Poor continuity of care

Potential Solution Areas:

* Natural Language Processing - NLP
* Clinical summarization
* Missing-information detection

---

# Communication

## Clinical Handoff Failures

Description:
Important patient information is lost during shift changes, referrals, transfers, or discharge.

Impact:

* Safety incidents
* Delayed care
* Readmissions

Potential Solution Areas:

* Handoff intelligence
* Automated summaries
* Risk dashboards

---

# Operations

## Hospital Workflow Bottlenecks

Description:
Inefficient patient movement, scheduling, staffing, and resource allocation.

Impact:

* Long wait times
* Staff burnout
* Increased costs

Potential Solution Areas:

* Workflow analytics
* Predictive scheduling
* Operational intelligence

---

# Climate & Resilience

## Operational Disruptions

Description:
Healthcare facilities face risks from extreme weather, power outages, flooding, heat events, and infrastructure failures.

Impact:

* Service interruptions
* Surgery cancellations
* Patient transfers

Potential Solution Areas:

* Resilience monitoring
* Predictive risk dashboards
* Resource planning

---

# Working Observation

Many healthcare problems are not caused by lack of data.

Many are caused by failure to deliver the right information, to the right person, at the right time, in a form that supports action.
