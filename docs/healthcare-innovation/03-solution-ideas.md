# Candidate Solutions

## Solution 1: Clinical Handoff Intelligence

Description:

An intelligent safety layer that verifies clinical handoffs, identifies missing information, tracks accountability, and ensures continuity of care during transitions.

Examples:

* Shift changes
* Ward transfers
* Emergency department transitions
* Hospital discharge

Potential Features:

* SBAR generation
* Missing information detection
* Follow-up ownership tracking
* Acknowledgement workflows
* Pending test result monitoring

Score: TBD
