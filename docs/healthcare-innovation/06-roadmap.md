# CareGuardian Roadmap

## Phase 1 – Discovery and Validation

Objective:

Confirm the highest-value healthcare problem and define a focused MVP.

Activities:

* Literature review
* NHS patient safety analysis
* Market research
* Competitor analysis
* Stakeholder mapping
* Problem prioritization

Deliverables:

* Problem Space Document
* Market Analysis Document
* Solution Prioritization Matrix
* CareGuardian Concept Definition

Status: Completed

---

## Phase 2 – MVP Definition

Objective:

Define the first CareGuardian product.

Current Candidate:

CareGuardian Clinical Handoff Intelligence

Key Questions:

* What information is commonly missed during handoffs?
* What information is commonly missing during discharge?
* What patient safety risks result?
* Which workflows should be supported first?

Potential Users:

* Nurses
* Physicians
* Patient Safety Officers
* Case Managers
* Discharge Coordinators

Deliverables:

* User Personas
* Use Cases
* Functional Requirements
* Non-Functional Requirements

Status: In Progress

---
