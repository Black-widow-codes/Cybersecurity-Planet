# User Personas

## Persona 1 – Sarah Thompson

### Role

Discharge Coordinator

### Work Environment

Large acute care hospital

### Responsibilities

* Coordinate patient discharges
* Ensure discharge summaries are complete
* Arrange follow-up appointments
* Communicate with primary care providers
* Coordinate with pharmacists and nursing staff
* Verify post-discharge plans

### Goals

* Ensure patients leave safely
* Reduce readmissions
* Ensure follow-up actions are completed
* Improve communication between care teams
* Prevent information from being lost during transitions

### Frustrations

* Missing discharge information
* Incomplete medication lists
* Delayed test results
* Unclear ownership of follow-up tasks
* Difficulty tracking whether receiving providers received information
* Manual review of multiple systems

### Risks

If information is missing:

* Patient may miss follow-up care
* Medication errors may occur
* Test results may be overlooked
* Readmissions may increase
* Patient safety incidents may occur

### How CareGuardian Helps

CareGuardian automatically:

* Detects missing information
* Identifies incomplete discharge summaries
* Flags missing medication information
* Tracks pending test results
* Verifies follow-up ownership
* Generates patient safety alerts

### Success Metrics

* Fewer incomplete discharges
* Reduced follow-up failures
* Improved communication
* Reduced preventable readmissions
* Improved patient safety outcomes
