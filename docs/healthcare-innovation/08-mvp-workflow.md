# CareGuardian MVP Workflow

## MVP Name

CareGuardian Clinical Handoff Intelligence

---

## Scenario

A patient is being discharged from hospital.

The care team must ensure that all critical information follows the patient safely.

---

## Current State

Patient Ready for Discharge

↓

Clinician completes discharge summary

↓

Discharge coordinator reviews information

↓

Follow-up actions are assigned

↓

Patient leaves hospital

↓

Potential problems:

* Missing diagnosis information
* Missing medication changes
* Missing follow-up appointments
* Missing pending test results
* Unclear ownership of tasks
* Communication failures

↓

Patient Harm Risk

---

## Future State with CareGuardian

Patient Ready for Discharge

↓

CareGuardian receives discharge information

↓

Completeness Check

Checks:

* Diagnosis present?
* Medication list present?
* Follow-up plan present?
* Pending tests documented?
* Responsible provider assigned?

↓

Risk Assessment

Low Risk
Medium Risk
High Risk

↓

Safety Alerts Generated

↓

Missing Information Corrected

↓

Discharge Approved

↓

Audit Trail Stored

↓

Patient Leaves Hospital

---

## Expected Benefits

* Fewer incomplete discharges
* Improved communication
* Better accountability
* Reduced preventable harm
* Reduced readmissions
* Improved patient safety

---

## MVP Success Metrics

* Number of missing items detected
* Number of corrected discharges
* Reduction in follow-up failures
* Reduction in information-related incidents
* User satisfaction
